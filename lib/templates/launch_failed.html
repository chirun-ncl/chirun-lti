Using macOS or iOS?

Due to restrictions enabled by default in the Safari browser NCL Coursebuilder content must be launched by loading it in a new tab. You can avoid this step by disabling cross-site tracking prevention and reloading the page.

macOS Catalina and later
  1. In the Safari app on your Mac, choose Safari > Preferences, then click Privacy.
  2. Untick "Prevent cross-site tracking".

iPhone/iPad

  1. Open the Settings app on your device.
  2. Scroll down and select Safari.
  3. Scroll down and turn off "Prevent Cross-Site Tracking".
