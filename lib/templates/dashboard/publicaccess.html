Public Access

By default content uploaded to the CourseBuilder LTI tool is protected by LTI authentication and must be accessed through the VLE (e.g. Blackboard or Canvas) using an account registered on the course. Sharing a direct link to the content will lead to authentication errors.

If you would like to allow learners to share direct links to this content, turn on the setting below. When enabled, the LTI authentication is not enforced and the content is publicly accessible to the world.
